Frequently asked questions

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve residential clients in the West Island of Montréal and nearby neighbourhoods. If you’re just outside this area, contact us and we’ll see what’s possible.

Is Xiveau a marketplace or the actual provider?

Xiveau manages and coordinates a network of vetted local professionals. You deal with us as your single contact — we handle scheduling, quality and payments with vendors.

Can I change or pause my plan?

Yes. Plans can typically be adjusted or paused with advance notice. We’ll walk you through options based on your services.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?

We believe in earning your trust, not locking you in. Terms depend on your services, but we favour flexible agreements.

Ready to put your home on autopilot?

Tell us a bit about your property and what you’d like to stop worrying about. We’ll follow up with a custom Xiveau plan and clear pricing.
